About Me

As a DOP, Director, and Producer with over 10 years in Ukrainian and international cinema, I craft narratives of hope in conflict zones. Inspired by Hoyte van Hoytema's mastery, my work emphasizes natural lighting, emotional depth, and simple yet powerful frames for art-house and mainstream. Member of the National Union of Cinematographers of Ukraine (NSKU, 1223 members strong). Education: Kyiv National University of Theatre, Film and TV. Languages: Ukrainian (native), English (fluent). Skills: ARRI/RED cameras, DaVinci Resolve color grading, DJI Ronin stabilization. Focused on co-productions UA-USA, UA-Europe for IDFA (Nov 13-23, 2025, Amsterdam) and NFF (Sep 26-Oct 3, 2025, Utrecht).

"Viacheslav's vision brought our story to life with boundless empathy and creative instincts." — Kyra Sedgwick, Narrator & EP, The Ark (World Premiere: Woodstock FF, Oct 2025)

Projects

Highlights from my portfolio, blending documentary hope with narrative power. Premieres 2025: Odesa IFF (Sep 24-Oct 4, Kyiv), Docudays UA (Jun 6-13, Kyiv), Molodist Kyiv IFF (Oct 25-Nov 2, Kyiv).

The Ark (2025)

Co-Dir/DOP: Ukrainian family saves 1000+ animals amid war. Natural light captures resilience. World Premiere: Woodstock FF; Nominated Leon Gast Award, Best Feature Doc.

I Work at the Cemetery (2021)

DOP: Black comedy on loss and redemption. Night setups with natural fog on Kyiv cemetery. Best Cinematography: Montelupo IIFF; Best Film: Munich IFA.

Silent Nava (In Dev.)

Dir/Prod: Narrative of quiet resistance in conflict. Focus on emotional depth via mono frames. Co-prod UA-Europe for IDFA 2025.

Kakhovka Object (2025)

DOP: Anti-Soviet action-doc on bunker horrors post-dam collapse. Natural lighting in flooded ruins. Premiere: Odesa IFF 2025.

Family Album (2024)

Prod: Photo-doc on Holodomor witnesses and war. Compositional minimalism for generational memory. Official Selection: Docudays UA 2025.

V temriavi (Short)

DOP: Experimental shadows in urban Kyiv. DaVinci grading for emotional contrast.
