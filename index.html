About

I’m Vyacheslav Rakovsky, DOP, director and producer from Kyiv, member of the Ukrainian Association of Cinematographers (derzkin.gov.ua). Since 2018 I turn reality into frames that change perspectives — from dark comedy on cemeteries to hope in shelters. My projects connect UA-Europe-USA with premieres at Warsaw IFF and Woodstock FF. I treat every setup as an act of faith. Available for DP work (IDFA co-prods) and brand stories. Let’s shoot your story together.
