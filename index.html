About Me

DOP, Director, and Producer with over 10 years crafting narratives of hope in conflict zones. Inspired by Hoyte van Hoytema — I use natural light, simple frames, and emotional depth for art-house and mainstream audiences.

Member: National Union of Cinematographers of Ukraine (NSKU)
Education: Kyiv National University of Theatre, Film and TV
Languages: Ukrainian (native), English (fluent)
Tools: ARRI • RED • DaVinci Resolve • DJI Ronin
